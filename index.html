Welcome to Massala

Newest Indian Takeaway in Ipswich - Massala. Why not give us a try?

That's our motto - Keep Calm & Curry On! Why not treat yourself to one of the nation's favourite dishes when you've had a hard day at work, or just have been playing hard!
